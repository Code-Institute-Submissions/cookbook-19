Find your perfect dish

The leading recipe sharing website. At the touch of a button you can have access to hundreds of recipes from across the world. With detailed instructions from other Cookbook community members you can easily create your perfect meal. You can easily check the ingredients for your ideal meal. Each recipe also contains vital information about allergies so you can easily check what to avoid. Whether you are after a light snack or a full blown dinner party there will be something for you.

Search

Browse through the collection of recipes. If you are pressed for time you can search by how long the dish takes to cook. Watching the waistline? Filter results by the number of calories. Take a culinary trip around the world and search for recipes based on their country of origin. Worried about allergies? Review search results to filter out recipes containing allergens. If you are a kitchen novice you can browse recipes by how difficult they are to cook. After a quick snack? Narrow down your search by finding specific meals. Or simply mark those recipes you love as your favourites.

Add

Do you have a special recipe that you come back to time and again? Add your own creation to the collection of recipes. You can specify ingredients, cooking time, calories and many other options to make cooking your favourite dish even easier.

Edit

Found a dish you like but it just needs that certain something extra? Simply edit the recipe to hone it to your taste. The changes you can make are limitless. Add extra ingredients, or go low carbs and reduce the number of calories. It is entirely up to you to customise.

Delete

Bored with a recipe or you just do not like fish? Delete recipes from the cookbook to avoid seeing them again.
